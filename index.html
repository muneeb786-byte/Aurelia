About AURELIA

AURELIA - The Luxury Hotel has been a landmark of exceptional hospitality since 1985. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail has made us a preferred destination for discerning travelers from around the world.

With 200 elegantly appointed rooms and suites, world-class dining options, and state-of-the-art facilities, we offer an unparalleled experience that combines timeless elegance with modern comfort.

Our dedicated team of hospitality professionals is committed to making every stay memorable, whether you're traveling for business or pleasure.
